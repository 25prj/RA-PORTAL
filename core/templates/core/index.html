TYPE APPROVAL DEALERSHIP LICENSE

WHY TYPE APPROVAL & DEALERSHIP LICENSE?

The NCA, under the Electronic Communications Act, 2008 (Act 775), mandates type approval and dealership licenses to ensure devices meet regulatory standards. This prevents substandard products, safeguards networks, and promotes fair trade by authorizing only approved dealers. These measures align Ghanaâ€™s telecommunications sector with international standards, ensuring safety and reliability.

Fair Trade

Authorizes only licensed dealers to sell communication devices, reducing the distribution of illegal or unapproved products.

SOME DEVICES TO CONSIDER FOR TYPE APPROVAL OR DEALERSHIP LICENSE

Mobile Phones

Mobile phones require type approval to ensure they comply with local communication standards, such as frequency bands and safety regulations.

Radio Transmitter

These devices are required to ensure they do not interfere with other communication services and operate within the prescribed frequencies and power limits.

Router

Wi-Fi devices need to be certified to ensure they do not cause interference with other communication devices and meet local wireless communication regulations.

Satelitte Communication Devices

Devices such as satellite phones or terminals require approval to ensure they comply with frequency allocation and other national communication rules.
